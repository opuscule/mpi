Machining

Micro Precision has a variety of state of the art horizontal and vertical milling tools meet the most stringent standards. We global experts in all forms of machining – from Prototype machining to CNC machining and turning to a wide range of CAD/CAM Capabilities.

  • We service long-term contracts
  • We specialize in Quick turnaround – our comprehensive MRP system means we can deliver products in a timely manner.
    • We can Vertical CNC Mill to 65 x 30 x 26 inches
    • Our Horizontal CNC Mill with Pallet changer's capacity ranges up to 28 x 28 x 30 inches
    • Our maximum turning capacity is 80 x 30 inches
    • Our CNC Boring Mill with Rotary Table is capable of machining 59 x 47 x 55 inches

Assembly

For the United States Navy Nuclear Submarine programs, Micro Precision provides complete manufacture, assembly and testing of critical components including, but not limited to:

  • Hydraulic Accumulators
  • Valves
  • Valve actuators
  • Manifold Assemblies
  • Weapons Handling equipment.

Materials

Our dedication to precision machining means we can fulfill your needs across a number of materials.

  • Our metals expertise covers a very wide range: From Aluminum, Bronze and Stainless Steel, to Titanium, Monel and other ferrous and non-ferrous metals.
  • We can also mill numerous types of plastics, should your needs call for them in your project.

Testing

Micro Precision has a variety of in-house testing capabilities to meet your critical demands. These include:

  • Non-Destructive Testing (NDT)
    • Visual Inspection (VT)
    • Liquid Penetrant Testing (PT) to detect surface defects such as hairline cracks, surface porosity
  • Two custom hydraulic and hydrostatic test stands
