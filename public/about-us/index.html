Micro Precision is proud to be an American company supporting some of our country's most important customers.

We make what matters. That's because our customers are, in a large part, dedicated to protecting America. We go to work every day promising to deliver components and assemblies that are simply too important to fail.

We've dedicated ourselves to that proposition for over 40 years. We're part of a financially secure organization whose investors believe in our role and goals.

Our uninterrupted years of success have enabled us to build a strong group of vendors and suppliers.

Our Mission

We strive every day to provide the highest quality components and assemblies to our nation's defense and homeland security customers.

Our Vision

To be the "first choice" every time by our unrelenting dedication to put our customers first.

Our Partnership

We have strong partnerships with Nautilus Integrated Solutions' subsidiary companies – PRL and Nathan Airchime. Our management team has deep experience serving in and supplying Military and Nuclear fields.

Our management team embraces our commitment to precision, quality and service.

Andrew Livingston

General Manager

Andrew assumed the role of General Manager in June 2021 following his retirement from the U.S. Navy. A nuclear-trained submariner; he served aboard three Los Angeles Class fast attack submarines — deploying several times in support of the Global War of Terror and national tasking unique to the Silent Service. He holds an undergraduate degree from Quinnipiac University.

Christian Kiraly

Program Manager

Christian joined Micro Precision in July 2022. As a Program Manager he is responsible for major business lines and leads internal data analytics. Prior to working at Micro Precision, Christian worked at Electric Boat Corporation as a Nuclear Test Engineer and Systems Engineer. He holds an undergraduate degree in Mechanical Engineering from Manhattan College.

John Gallonio

Manufacturing Manager

John joined Micro Precision in October 2022 and leads all machining operations. He began his career as a progressive tool and die maker and brings 15 years of CNC and wire EDM experience to the team.

Zachary Potter

Program Manager

Zack joined Micro Precision in November 2022 after serving six years in the U.S. Navy Submarine Force. He is responsible for all operations and production for Nathan Airchime (a subsidiary of Micro Precision).

Bill Piotroski

Production Manager

Bill joined the Micro Precision in July 2020. As Production Manager, he is responsible for complex assembly and test and overall shop production. Bill has over 30 years of experience in industrial design and manufacturing. Bill holds an undergraduate degree in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Connecticut.

Stuart Alexander

Contracts Manager

Stuart joined Micro Precision in 1990. Over his 30+ year career he served as a machinist, quality inspector, and Quality Manager before his current role as Contract Manger in 2000. Stuart’s wealth of knowledge and experience is vital to Micro Precision’s success and growth.

Christy Valle

Human Resources/Office Manager

Christy joined Micro Precision in 2018 as an administrative assistant. She is now responsible for Human Resource operations including recruiting, benefits, payroll, employee safety, employee reviews, and maintaining company policies and procedures. Bringing 20+ years of retail management experience, Christy is able to nurture a positive working environment, and maintain management and employee relations.
