PRL is proud to be an American company dedicated to supplying some of America’s most important customers.

PRL is dedicated to serving customers working to defend America. We understand the importance of delivering castings and machined components where failure is not an option.

This has been our philosophy for over 40 years. Our company has multiple facilities with overlapping capabilities. Our financial status is healthy – we’re backed by strong investors who believe in our management philosophy. We engage in cross training programs for critical roles to ensure your needs are always met.

Our history of success has enabled us to create and maintain a strong network of vendors, suppliers, and subcontractors.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional quality components allowing peace of mind for our nation’s military, nuclear, and industrial applications.

Our Vision

To be the "Exception to the Rule" by striving to be the most innovative, disciplined, and talented manufacturer of high specification components.

Our Partnership

We have strong partnerships with Nautilus Integrated Solutions' subsidiary companies — Micro Precision and Nathan Airchime.

Our leaders embrace our commitment to precision, quality, and service.

Our management team has deep experience serving in and supplying Military and Nuclear fields.
