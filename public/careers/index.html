Micro Precision offers you the opportunity to grow your career while working for a company that makes things that matter.

Why Micro Precision?

Micro Precision is a company with a history of success, an outstanding present, and a bright future. We're a small company making critical components and assemblies for the United States Navy's Nuclear Submarine fleet. Those products help keep sailors and our country safe. We pride ourselves on providing you with opportunities for personal and professional growth and satisfaction. We encourage continuous improvement – for our products and our employees.

At Micro Precision, LLC, we value a diverse, inclusive workforce and we provide equal employment opportunity for all applicants and employees. All qualified applicants for employment will be considered without regard to an individual’s race, color, sex, gender identity, gender expression, religion, age, national origin or ancestry, citizenship, physical or mental disability, medical condition, family care status, marital status, domestic partner status, sexual orientation, genetic information, military or veteran status, or any other basis protected by federal, state or local laws. If you are unable to submit your application because of incompatible assistive technology or a disability, please contact us at hr@nautilusis.com. Micro Precision, LLC will reasonably accommodate qualified individuals with disabilities to the extent required by applicable law.

Our Values

At Micro Precision, we're proud of our ability to provide precision products to customers with the highest level of expectations. We understand their importance – and to your contribution to their excellence.

Culture

Micro Precision works hard to promote a culture of respect, ethics, mutual respect in an atmosphere of goodwill. We're small enough to promote a collaborative work environment that is both casual and customer oriented. To that end, we offer flexible working hours, and, where possible, the option of remote work.

Training & Mentorship

We offer training on our MAZAK CNC machine tools. You benefit from our membership in the American Management Association and our connection with EWIB (Eastern Connecticut Workforce Investment Board) Our business is guided by input, insights and training from Connstep – Connecticut's leading business consulting firm.

Benefits

  • Health Insurance
  • Vision Insurance
  • Tuition Reimbursement
  • Free Parking
  • Dental Insurance
  • 401k
  • Short- and long-term Disability Insurance
